Our Mission: To spread awareness about the spiritual oppresion people face from mainstream bourgeoisie culuture and further our own journey toward liberation. We achieve this through our weekly marxist book club and our self-expression through coffee, tea and matcha.

Our Medium: Through the choices we provide our customers we grant them a taste of freedom which allows them to realize the depths of the capitalist chains that bind them. Self analysis is a prerequisite for true liberation and so we provide a privilege in addition to every order.

Our Milk: Alternative milks are the esential life force that motivates us. They are all produced ethically and sustainably in house. The painstaking labor of aquiring these milks is transformed and transported to our customers who gain an understanding of the value of their own labor and the exploitation that underpins the capitalist system.
